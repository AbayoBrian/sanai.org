IRRIGATION, DRILLING AND WATER HARVESTING

Irrigation is the artificial application of controlled amounts of water to agricultural crops and landscapes. We design, supply and install irrigation systems and fittings for crop production and lawn maintenance. We design and install both overhead and surface irrigation and also supply pumps, pipes and fittings for low cost surface irrigation through canals or basins. Our integrated management and market linkage services also extend to open field produced crops.

Water is the life sustaining component of any agricultural production venture. We therefore endeavor to ensure that our farmers have access to sufficient, good quality water for crop and animal production. Our greenhouses are designed to have gutters for water harvesting. We also provide dam liners for lining water harvesting dams. Our borehole services include hydro geological survey, drilling and extraction permit acquisition, drilling, test pumping and borehole equipping.
